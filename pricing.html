Our Pricing

We Use a One of a Kind Pricing Model

To ensure you pay for exactly the work is done, we charge on a per-minute basis (similar to cell phone plans). During your booking, you estimate the amount of time needed for the job with the help of our web tool. If the job doesn't take as long as estimated, you will simply be refunded the difference. If the job takes a little longer than anticipated, simply pay for the remaining work.
